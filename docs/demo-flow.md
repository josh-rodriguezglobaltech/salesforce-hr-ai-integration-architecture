# Demo Flow

The demo presents a simple but complete HR integration scenario.

## Scenario

A recruiter or HR operations user enters a candidate into a synthetic HRIS interface.

The system then:

1. Stores the candidate in the HR database.
2. Generates an AI recruiter summary.
3. Publishes the enriched candidate record into Salesforce.
4. Displays the candidate in Salesforce with the AI summary populated.

## Demo Steps

1. Open the application landing page.
2. Authenticate with Salesforce.
3. Enter a new candidate in the Synthetic HRIS Candidate Intake form.
4. Submit the candidate.
5. Review the success confirmation.
6. Open Salesforce.
7. Navigate to the Candidates tab.
8. Open the new Candidate record.
9. Review the AI Summary field.

## Business Value

This demo shows how an enterprise integration layer can connect HR systems, AI services, and Salesforce to create a streamlined candidate management workflow.
